About CaseMatrix AI

🤖 How It Works

Our system uses state-of-the-art Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) technology to find the most relevant clinical cases from our database. Your query is converted to semantic embeddings and compared with thousands of case vectors to find the best matches.

🔬 Technology Stack

Built with cutting-edge technologies: Python Flask backend, ChromaDB vector database, Sentence Transformers for embeddings, and a responsive Bootstrap frontend for seamless user experience.

⚕️ Clinical Focus

Our database contains real anonymized clinical cases covering various medical specialties. Each case includes symptoms, diagnosis, treatment approaches, and patient demographics.

⚖️ Disclaimer

This tool is for educational and research purposes only. Always consult with qualified healthcare professionals for actual patient care and clinical decision-making.
